---
title: Primitives
description: Single-purpose animation building blocks for Remotion.
---

Primitives are small, focused components — one animation, one effect, one purpose. Compose them into scenes, or use them directly inside a `<Composition>`.

## Available

- [Blur Reveal](/docs/primitives/blur-reveal) — text fades in from a heavy blur.

## Coming soon

- `Typewriter` — character-by-character text reveal with a deterministic blinking cursor.
- `InlineHighlight` — color sweep across a span inside a sentence.
- `TextFadeReplace` — swap one line of text for another in place.
